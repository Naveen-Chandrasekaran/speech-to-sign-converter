About the Project

This project is an AI-powered speech-to-sign language interpreter built to bridge communication between the hearing and speech impaired and the wider world. It converts spoken language into real-time sign language animations using 3D avatars.

Our Mission

To leverage technology for inclusive communication and improve accessibility for everyone, especially those in the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform in real-time sign language translation, making the world more connected and accessible.
